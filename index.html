Smart IoT-Based Water Quality Monitoring

RuralReact is an advanced ESP32-based IoT device designed for real-time water quality monitoring. It senses and collects data including pH, ORP, Temperature, and Electrical Conductivity (EC) and sends it to a mobile application.

How It Works

The RuralReact device is powered by ESP32 microcontroller technology. It collects sensor data and transmits it wirelessly to a dedicated mobile application. Users can monitor live water quality parameters, view historical data, and receive alerts if water conditions exceed safe limits.

Mobile Application Integration

Our IoT-based mobile application provides real-time dashboards, graphical reports, and notifications. This enables rural communities, farmers, and water treatment systems to ensure clean and safe water.
